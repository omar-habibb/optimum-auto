Our Service


Every wash we deliver is built on one simple promise: clean, safe, and consistent care for your car’s exterior. Every wash is carried out by our trained team, using quiet, low-noise tools that respect both your peace and your environment. Your car is cleaned exactly where it’s parked — no keys, no movement, no hassle. We use specialized tools at every step, from high-quality sprayers to microfiber towels made specifically for rinsing, drying, and even glass. This level of care ensures a safe, scratch-free clean that protects your paint and delivers a flawless, streak-free finish every time.


Subscription Plans

Optimum Auto offers two simple, flexible subscription options designed to keep your car consistently clean — with professional service brought directly to your location.

  • 6 Days a Week Plan

    • The perfect match for daily use. Unmatched presence, every single day.

  • 3 Days a Week Plan

    • Ideal for light to moderate use. Staying fresh without the daily routine.

Each subscription runs on a 4-week cycle. You choose the plan that fits your needs, and we handle the rest with precision, consistency, and care.


Freezing Your Subscription

If you ever need to pause your service, we offer a one-week freeze option. You can request a freeze once every subscription cycle, and your current plan will be extended by one week. To freeze your subscription, simply notify us via WhatsApp.
